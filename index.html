My Top 5 Personal Values

by Royce • Fall 2025

Authenticity

  • I strive to be genuine in my words and actions.
  • I stay true to my beliefs, even under pressure.
  • I value transparency and honesty in relationships.

Determination

  • I pursue my goals with persistence and focus.
  • I embrace challenges as opportunities to grow stronger.
  • I don’t give up when things get tough—I push through.

Kindness

  • I treat others with compassion and empathy.
  • I offer help without expecting anything in return.
  • I believe small acts of kindness can make a big impact.

Diligence

  • I approach tasks with care, precision, and purpose.
  • I value doing things right—not just quickly.
  • Diligence reflects my commitment to excellence and follow-through.

Maturity

  • I take responsibility for my actions and choices.
  • I reflect before reacting and consider others’ perspectives.
  • Maturity is the thread that weaves together authenticity, determination, diligence, and kindness—it’s how I show up with wisdom and balance.
